📋 Family Fund — Circle Policy
Jun 1, 2026 · Approved by all 5 members
Ratified
Contribution rules

Each member contributes $200 on the 1st of every month via Mekseb. Payments 5+ days late incur a $10 fee. After 30 days the admin reviews and may suspend the member pending a group vote.

Withdrawal policy

Withdrawals require approval from at least 4 of 5 members via in-app vote. Emergency withdrawals (medical, bereavement) are granted within 24 hours. All planned withdrawals require 14 days notice.

Quitting the circle

A member wishing to leave must give 30 days written notice. Their final month's contribution is forfeited. All prior savings are returned within 14 business days of departure.

Adding new members

New members require unanimous approval. Applicants must pass ID verification, provide income proof, and meet the qualification checklist. A 3-month probationary period applies.

Dispute resolution

Disputes go to a group vote first. If unresolved within 7 days, Mekseb's mediation team is engaged. Their ruling is final and binding for all members.

Want to propose a policy change?
Changes to the policy require a group vote. All members will be notified.
