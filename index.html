Why Mekseb

Built for real communities

Every feature was designed around one idea: money is better when it moves together.

👥

Private savings circles

Create a closed group with custom rules, monthly targets, and full privacy. Your circle, your terms.

💳

Seamless card payments

Every member contributes directly from their card. Payments tracked and confirmed in real time.

📊

Live payment tracking

See exactly who paid, who's pending, and the running total — updated the moment a payment clears.

🔔

Automatic reminders

The system sends monthly reminders so you don't have to. Email, SMS, and in-app — automatically.

🌍

Multi-country, multi-currency

Members from different countries? Mekseb handles cross-border payments and currency conversion.

📋

Group-written policy

Every circle sets its own rules — withdrawals, quitting, adding members — enforced by the platform.
